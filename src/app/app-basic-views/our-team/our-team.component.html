MEET THE FOUNDER AND THE SUPPORT STAFF.

Mr. Monakali started his articles of Clerkship in 2011 with Mavonya Moshesh & Associates, a medium law firm in Butterworth in Eastern Cape.

In 2012 he was officially admitted as an attorney of the High Court after having met all the requirements for admission as an attorney in South Africa.

After his admission as an attorney Mr. Monakali served as a professional legal assistant in the same Law firm. In 2013 he was employed by Road Accident Fund as a Claim’s Officer.

In 2018 he decided to resign at Road Accident Fund in order to open his own law firm for personal and professional growth and development and to serve the legal service needs of the community.
